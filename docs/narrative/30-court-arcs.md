# Court Arcs

Storylines around 女官 and the 朝堂.

## Hooks

- **上朝 (朝会)** — the 朝堂 free-view action; a natural cadence for governance beats
  that nudge `authority` / `publicSupport` / `factionPressure`.
- **司礼女官 (`sili_nvguan`)** — ritual/ceremony officer; drives 经血祭仪 and protocol
  arcs. Favor term is 圣眷 (officials), not 恩宠.
- **Tension with the 后宫** — officials resisting "内宠干政" (consorts influencing
  policy) is a renewable conflict.

## Patterns with current tools

- Court numbers are dials moved by `resource`/`court` effects; there's no faction
  simulation, so stage faction conflict as **scripted events** with number nudges.
- Use `eventFired` + flags to sequence a multi-session court storyline.
- A man secretly studying 兵法/律令 (forbidden) is a strong court/scandal beat —
  reveal via flags and memory, judge via choices.
