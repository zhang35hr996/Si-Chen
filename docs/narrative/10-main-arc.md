# Main Arc

The player is the 女帝 (or a high-ranking woman), forced to trade across three
resources: **江山 (realm), 后宫 (harem), 血脉 (bloodline).**

## The spine

Every major choice can hit politics, affection, lineage, and ritual legitimacy at
once. The throughline: the sovereign's reproductive/mandate state (经血) is public
and political, so private choices in the 后宫 ripple into the 朝堂 and the 宗室.

## Three pressures

- **江山** — 朝政, 军权, 税赋, 地方, official factions, 宗室 balance, 民心/威望.
- **后宫** — favor, 位分 promotion, 侍寝, rivalry, family marriage, 承养 competition,
  men's mood & loyalty.
- **血脉** — 经血祭祀, 怀胎 choice (自孕/承养), 胎息 stability, 承养人 health, heir growth,
  succession order.

## Staging for the current build

The engine supports scripted events/scenes, favor/relationship/memory, court &
bloodline numbers, and a 宫城/后宫/京城/郊外 map. Build the early main arc from those:
introduce key consorts/officials, let favor & memory accumulate, and use court /
bloodline number shifts as stakes. Defer 承养/继承 machinery (not implemented) to
scripted, flavor-level beats — see
[`../systems/60-pregnancy-and-heir-system.md`](../systems/60-pregnancy-and-heir-system.md).
