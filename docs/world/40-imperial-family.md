# Imperial Family

The setting deliberately redefines kinship words so "子/儿/主" don't default to male.

## The sovereign's own children

| Identity | Base / minor title | Adult title | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| all offspring (collective) | 皇嗣 | — | legal term, gender-neutral |
| the sovereign's daughters | 皇子 | 凤主 | 皇子 is legally neutral but socially female-coded; 公主 is a deprecated term |
| the sovereign's sons | 皇郎 | 贵主 | male heirs; 贵主 only after formal 下赘 + enfeoffment |

- **皇子** — formal offspring of the sovereign, legally not sex-specific; socially
  defaults to daughters, who hold succession, fief, and political subjecthood.
- **凤主** — an adult, enfeoffed female 皇子 with land, household troops, and some
  administrative power. Closer to "藩主/主公" than to any delicate connotation. 公主 is the deprecated old term.
- **皇郎 / 贵主** — male offspring; 贵主 is the honorific only after a 皇郎 formally marries out (下赘) and is enfeoffed — not automatic on adulthood.

## Imperial kin & branch lines

| Identity | Title | Note |
|---|---|---|
| sovereign's sisters | 长凤主 / 皇姊 / 皇妹 | adult kinswomen, may hold land & standing |
| sisters' children | 皇侄 | significant matrilineal branch |
| sisters' adult daughters | 宗主 / 郡主 | enfeoffed by standing |
| sisters' adult sons | 宗郎 / 宗君 | kin courtesy, no core power |
| kin via imperial men (承养/marriage), off the main matriline | 皇甥 / 宗甥 / 外宗 | below 皇子/皇郎/皇侄; usually outside the core succession line |

## Succession logic (worldbuilding level)

Imperial standing follows the **female** blood source, not male 承养 ties:

> 皇室身份与政治重要性主要看女性血源，不看男性承养关系。

The precise succession rule (eldest daughter / merit / 经血 strength / priestly
confirmation / hybrid) is **【待定】** — do not encode a definite rule in content.

The 东宫 (heir-apparent) consort system (东宫正君/贵侍/侍君/小侍) exists in lore but
is not yet modeled in game.
