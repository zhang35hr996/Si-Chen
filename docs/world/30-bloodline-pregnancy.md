# Bloodline & Pregnancy (worldbuilding)

## Two paths to bear

- **自孕制 (self-pregnancy):** a woman carries to term herself. Lineage is purely
  matrilineal; no male 承养 involved. Politically notable for the sovereign because
  it bypasses 后宫 factions.
- **承养制 (blood-nursing):** after a period of pregnancy, the 胎息 may be transferred
  to a chosen man, who sustains it with his own blood until birth.

## 胎息 and 血养

- **胎息** — the gestating life (medical + mystical connotation).
- **血养** — nourishing the 胎息 with blood. Core rule: once a 承养人 is named, the
  bond is **irreplaceable** — no substitute man, no woman, no stored/animal blood;
  abandoning or secretly transferring a 胎息 is a grave crime. This irreplaceability
  is what makes a man's 承养 qualification a political resource.
- Long-term 血养 weakens the man (气血亏损), even shortening life — the
  consumptive cost men bear.

## Menstrual sanctity & legitimacy

A woman's 经血 signifies fertility, mandate, and ancestral legitimacy. For the
sovereign it is a **state** matter:
- Regular 经血 ⇒ the realm's foundation is stable.
- Irregular/absent ⇒ priests, kin, and court take notice.
- Confirmed 绝经 ⇒ the mandate is shifting; succession should begin.

Pregnancy-absence and short post-partum absence are **not** 绝经. The criterion for
confirming 绝经 (one year / three years / priestly rite) is **【待定】**.
